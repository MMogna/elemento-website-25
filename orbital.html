Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Orbital servers and deployment

What's included with Orbital servers?

Each Orbital server includes the hardware, AtomOS pre-installed with perpetual licensing, and comprehensive support. No additional software licensing required.

Are Orbital servers only for on-premise deployment?

Yes, Orbital servers are designed exclusively for on-premise deployment in your own data center or colocation facilities. This ensures maximum control, security, and compliance.

Can I customize Orbital configurations?

Yes, we offer custom configurations through our Orbital Custom option. Our team will work with you to design the perfect solution for your specific requirements.

What support is included?

All Orbital servers include comprehensive hardware and software support, remote management capabilities, and access to our technical team.

How does the perpetual licensing work?

AtomOS perpetual licensing means you own the software license forever with no recurring fees. Updates and support are included for the life of the server.

What are the benefits of on-premise deployment?

On-premise deployment provides data sovereignty, eliminates latency, ensures compliance, and offers predictable costs with no variable cloud pricing.
